My Role:

I joined the founding team of the Hardware & Sanitary category at Udaan.com. My role was to help in understanding the viability of hardware business for the company, define a roadmap for the launch and launch the category.

Journey:

How we started launching a category in 4 months by doing the following steps

  • Industry Overview
  • Product/ Category – Market fit
  • Launch Plan
  • Business Plan

Industry Overview:

  • Conducted market research to identify the TAM,SAM & SOM of the Indian Sanitary & Hardware industry
  • Identified the Supply clusters for the Indian hardware industry across all the sub categories
  • Identified the top brands, share of branded & unbranded business, supply chain of all the sub categories

Product – Market fit

Customer Interview:

Conducted customer interview to understand their pain points and how they are currently solving them

Supplier Interview:

Business Development:

As a Management trainee of Udaan.com, B2B e-commerce startup, I have been exposed to multiple departments to get an understanding of how an e-commerce works. As a business developer, I was responsible for expanding the Electrical category which was launched 5 months ahead. Scaled up the business from 25L to 1Cr in 4months with the help of sales, supply & merchandizing teams Journey:

  • Studied the Electrical product portfolio, and the electrical industry nuances
  • Visited markets across geographies to understand the customers shopping behavior, pain points and competitors proposition.
  • Recruited sales team and gave category training to them, this helped the category to expand fast with a larger team
  • Designed a system to generate Permanent Journey Plan (PJP) for salesmen based on the outlets located in each pin code and distance between them
  • Gave insights to the supply team on the threshold price points by doing analysis on the current offered price, market needed price & competitor offered price
  • Experimented with different pricing slabs to move volumes during sale events
  • Designed and maintained dashboards to track the Customer funnel and to analyze the significant drop-offs at each stage
  • Set targets for the sales team by considering their previous months GMV and customers count achieved and projected the overall category targets
  • Launched coins scheme to attract new customers to the platform and to increase the organic footfall
  • Piloted a project to test the organic customers growth by tele calling them and helping them understand the offers and product portfolio
  • After analyzing customer painpoints and realizing the impact credit has on their purchasing patterns, introduced credit facility for electrical customers
  • Prepared a roadmap for increasing credit penetration in customers and motivated the sales team in completing the KYC activity to activate credit lines
  • Increase in Returns affected vendors and increased the transit costs of the warehouse team. Launched a program to reduce the Return to Origin ( RTO ) by streamlining communication between warehouse and the sales team

Accomplishments:

  • Expanded the sales team from 25-120 in 12 months by recruiting and training
  • Credit lines have been activated for 30% of the customer base within 5 months, resulting in doubling the ABS of activated customers
  • Maintained data transparency to sales team and senior management by building and maintaining dashboards
  • Reduced RTO from 14% to 8%
  • Category customer base and revenues grew month-on-month by 20% and 35% respectively and hit INR 1 crore revenue in 4 months of expansion.
